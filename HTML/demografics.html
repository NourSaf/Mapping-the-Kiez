About
DEMOGRAPHICAL DISTRIBUTION
presents a collection of photographs capturing the essence of Bergmannstraße, a vibrant street in Berlin's Bergmannkiez neighborhood. This series offers a ground-level perspective, showcasing the architectural diversity, commercial vibrancy, and cultural richness of the area. Each image documents various facets of the street life, from the bustling cafés and shops to the serene moments of everyday life. The collection serves as a visual archive, providing insights into the urban landscape and the community's dynamics. Through detailed imagery, the project aims to convey the unique character and atmosphere of Bergmannstraße, reflecting its significance within Berlin's urban tapestry.
