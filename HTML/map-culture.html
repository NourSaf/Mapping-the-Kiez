Chronological Cartography of Berlin's Bergmannkiez
The interactive map is a dynamic visualization tool designed to explore the multifaceted landscape of a street, showcasing a variety of categories that make up its vibrant ecosystem. It allows users to navigate through distinct sections such as Retail Trade, offering insights into local shops and boutiques; Food, highlighting restaurants and cafes; Health Care, pinpointing medical facilities and pharmacies; Supermarkets, for grocery shopping locations; Spätis, revealing the uniquely Berlin convenience stores that stay open late; Social Places, which includes bars, clubs, and gathering spots; and Services, covering a range of utilities from banking to laundry. This map serves as a comprehensive guide, enabling users to discover and interact with the street's social, cultural, and economic offerings, enriching their understanding and experience of the area.
