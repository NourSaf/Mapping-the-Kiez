Chronological Cartography of Berlin's Bergmannkiez
This collection serves as a comprehensive visual archive, documenting the district's architectural, environmental, and urban transitions over more than two centuries. Each map is presented in chronological order, providing a clear, visual progression of the changes and developments within Bergmannkiez. The archive is designed to offer insights into the historical layers that have contributed to the area's current urban landscape, making it a valuable resource for researchers, students, and enthusiasts of urban geography and historical cartography. Through this cartographic retrospective, viewers can trace the evolution of Bergmannkiez, gaining a deeper understanding of the factors that have influenced its development through time.
