The "Streetscape" project presents a multifaceted exploration of the Bergmannkiez neighborhood in Berlin, combining cartographic representations, photographic documentation, and demographic data analysis to offer a comprehensive visualization of this urban area. By integrating detailed maps and vivid street-level photographs, the project captures the unique architectural and cultural ambiance of Bergmannkiez. Concurrently, it employs demographic research to provide insights into the population dynamics, socio-economic profiles, and cultural diversity of the area's inhabitants. This methodological triangulation not only enhances our understanding of the physical and social fabric of Bergmannkiez but also serves as a model for urban studies, highlighting the importance of interdisciplinary approaches in capturing the complexity of cityscapes. Through its innovative integration of visual and statistical data, "Streetscape" contributes significantly to the field of urban geography by offering a richer, more nuanced portrayal of urban life and space.
